Security Infrastructure

Last Updated: 2026-04-16

1. Data Encryption

Security is our top priority. All data transmitted between your local client and our infrastructure is heavily encrypted via SSL/TLS. Passwords and sensitive PII are hashed using industry standard algorithms such as bcrypt.

2. Compliance & Standards

We follow SOC2 and ISO guidelines internally to ensure that any software modifications, deployments, and access control lists are continuously regulated and audited.
